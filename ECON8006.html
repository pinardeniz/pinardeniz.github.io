ECON8006

ECON 8006 - (PhD) - Fall Term Course

This course consists of independent modules. Each module is self-contained and guest lecturers are invited. The course intends to make the students familiar with the selected methods and applications in econometrics and statistics. Readings and other course documents will be announced prior to each module. Missing a lecture means failing. There will be several homework regarding the topics covered.

Class Hours: FRI 19.00 (GMT+03:00)

Class: Online

Suggested Reading List
James D. Hamilton - Time Series Analysis
James Davidson - Econometric Theory
Jeffrey M. Wooldridge - Econometric analysis of cross section and panel data
A. Colin Cameron and Pravin K. Trivedi - Microeconometrics Methods and Applications

Suggested Websites
https://www.ssc.wisc.edu/~bhansen/econometrics/
